Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 28, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Cal Poly Construction Management (CM) mobile application ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information. By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, school year, role (student/faculty/admin)
  • Preferences: Clubs joined, favorite companies, event interests
  • User-Generated Content: Event check-ins, club memberships, requests

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device type, OS, identifiers)
  • Usage data (features used, pages viewed, time spent)
  • FCM tokens for push notifications
  • Log data (IP address, access times, crash reports)

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Google Sign-In profile information
  • Firebase authentication and database services

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Uses

  • Provide and maintain App functionality
  • Authenticate users and manage accounts
  • Send relevant notifications
  • Display events, clubs, and company data
  • Track event attendance
  • Facilitate CM community connections

3.2 Secondary Uses

  • Improve performance and user experience
  • Analyze anonymized usage trends
  • Respond to support requests
  • Prevent fraud and security issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

3.3 We Do Not

  • Sell personal information
  • Use data for unrelated marketing
  • Share data with advertisers
  • Track location without consent

4. How We Share Your Information

Information may be shared with event organizers, club admins, service providers (such as Google Firebase), Cal Poly for administrative purposes, or when legally required.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted passwords, HTTPS/TLS, Firebase security rules, and regular audits. No system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and update your profile
  • Delete your account
  • Manage notification preferences
  • Request data portability
  • Object to certain data uses

7. Cookies and Tracking

The App uses local storage and Firebase Analytics. Analytics data is anonymized. Google services follow their own privacy policies.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. International Data Transfers

Data is stored on Firebase servers and may be transferred to the United States in compliance with applicable laws.

10. Data Retention

Data is retained while accounts are active and deleted according to the timelines described in this policy.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have rights to know, delete, and access personal information. We do not sell personal data.

12. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

Users in the EEA have GDPR rights including access, rectification, erasure, and data portability.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of changes.
